Located in Athens, Greece, it has an excessive inquisitiveness about printed matters and typography. Through observation and a process of trial and error — using both analogue and digital methods — it develops assorted visual systems. With several years of experience in various design agencies, and involvement in projects for a diverse range of clients from Greece and abroad, the practice has cultivated a versatile and adaptive approach to design. Each project is approached with curiosity, careful research, and a balance between conceptual thinking and functional execution. The design process places equal weight on clarity, experimentation, and storytelling, resulting in work that is both thoughtful and communicative.
